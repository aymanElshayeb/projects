Shuttle Bus Route Today

"Shuttle Bus Route Today" application is a mobile applications that can be used by any company that uses its own shuttle busses to deliver its employees to their homes. The application helps the company to organize the routes of the buses dynamically every day so that company can save employees time and also saving its money by cancelling unnecessary busses trips. There are 2 main actors to application. Employee and Bus Driver.

For Employee: He will have the application in his mobile. He should confirm every night whether he will use the bus tomorrow or not . For Bus Driver: He will have the application in his mobile. Every morning he will see an Google map contains the employees' meeting points today. He should decide the best path to collect them. The company may decide to cancel the trip and joining the lines. Driver's mobile should also send bus location during its trip so that employee can see the current route of the bus. This page is simulation to the mobile application.
